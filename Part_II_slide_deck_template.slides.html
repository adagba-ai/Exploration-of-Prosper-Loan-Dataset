Investigation Overview¶

Following a carefull analysis of Prosper Loan Dataset, our major concern was to predict the successful outcome of an application for a loan from Prosper, and factors such as Recommendation, EmploymentStatus, IsBorrowerHomeowner, Occupation, and IncomeVerifiable were analyzed. The following are summary of key insights gotten from our analysis:

0 accounted for the highest number of Recommendationhis. This signifies that having a high number of Recommendation does not guarantee getting a loan. Other factors could have been considered.

over 60,000 Borrowers are employed. This is a good indication that Proper consider mostly employed people for their loan. Its common to note that their reason for this could easily be that only people with jobs can repay their loan.

Borrowers whose income were verifiable had a better chance of getting Prosper loan compared to those who were not verified.

Borrowers who were Not-employed had their income verified the most. Followed by those who were self-employed. We could also see that for Borrowers who were employed, there was little verification of income. These analysis goes further to justify our earlier claim that Prosper could have been more interested in Borrowers who were employed, hence will definately verify the income of those who are not employed.

Surprisingly, Others in the Category of Occupation had a better chance of getting Prosper Loan with a count or frequency of 28000. Another group is Proffessional with a frequency of 14000. Computer Programmer-Lucky we, had a frequncy of 4000, indicating a good chance to get prosper loan as a Computer Programmer. Student had the least chance of getting a loan from Prosper.

Dataset Overview¶

Prosper loan dataset contains 113,937 loans with 81 variables on each loan, including loan amount, borrower rate (or interest rate), current loan status, borrower income, and many others.
